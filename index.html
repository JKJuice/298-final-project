About Me

I started swimming competitively at local swim club called Novaquatics since I was seven, and have swam ever since. I have made into national-level qualifying competitions such as Futures Championships, Junior Nationals, Junior Olympics, and Speedo Sectionals and won top three medals at those competitions. I am also certified as a coach on the USA Swimming organization, and have been trained with all the necessary qualifications such as CPR training, Safe Sport, etc.
